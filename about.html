Hello and welcome! I'm Pavana, and this is a website for people like me, who can't cook. I created "Cook It Yourself" to provide quick and easy recipes for those who lack culinary skills. Now, I'm the type of person who can mess up making even the simplest foods, like waffles for example. I found these recipes that even I can't mess up! I hope you give them a try, and realize for yourself, that even you can create visually pleasing, delcious food!
